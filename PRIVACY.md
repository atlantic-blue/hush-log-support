# Privacy Policy

**HushLog** — Atlantic Blue Solutions LTD
**Effective date:** 1 March 2026

## Overview

HushLog is a snoring analysis app that records and analyses audio while you sleep. All data stays on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

## Data We Collect

**None.** HushLog does not collect any data from your device.

## Data Stored On Your Device

The following data is created and stored locally on your device only:

- **Audio recordings** of your sleep sessions
- **Analysis results** including snoring amplitude, classifications, and session summaries
- **Session history** for tracking trends over time

This data is stored using on-device storage (SQLite) and the device file system. It is never transmitted off your device.

## Microphone Access

HushLog requires microphone access to record audio during sleep sessions. The microphone is only active when you explicitly start a recording session. Audio data captured by the microphone is stored locally and is never sent to any server.

## Third-Party Services

HushLog does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no SDKs that collect or transmit user data.

## Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. There is no data to share — everything stays on your device.

## Data Retention and Deletion

All data is stored on your device. You can delete individual sessions within the app or remove all data by uninstalling the app.

## Children's Privacy

HushLog does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version here with an updated effective date.
