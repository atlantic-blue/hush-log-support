# HushLog Support

Welcome to the support page for **HushLog** — a sleep snoring recorder and analyser for iOS.

## About HushLog

HushLog records and analyses your snoring while you sleep. All data stays on your device — we do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

### Features

- Record audio during sleep sessions with background recording support
- Automatic snoring detection and classification (quiet, light, loud, epic)
- Session scoring and trend tracking over time
- Track remedies and lifestyle factors that affect your snoring

### Common Questions

**Q: Does HushLog send my recordings to the cloud?**
A: No. All audio recordings and analysis data are stored locally on your device. Nothing is transmitted externally.

**Q: Why does HushLog need microphone access?**
A: HushLog uses the microphone to record audio while you sleep so it can detect and analyse snoring. The microphone is only active when you explicitly start a recording session.

**Q: Why does HushLog need to run in the background?**
A: Sleep sessions typically last several hours. Background audio allows HushLog to continue recording after you lock your device or switch to another app.

**Q: How do I delete my data?**
A: You can delete individual sessions within the app. To remove all data, uninstall the app.
