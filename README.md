  ## FAQ

  **How does Hush Log record my sleep?**
  Hush Log uses your iPhone's microphone to record audio overnight and analyses amplitude levels to classify snoring
  intensity.

  **Does my audio get uploaded anywhere?**
  No. All recordings and analysis stay entirely on your device. Nothing is sent to the cloud.

  **Can I export my data?**
  Data export is planned for a future update.

  **What if the app isn't detecting my snoring?**
  Make sure your phone is placed on your bedside table with the microphone unobstructed. Avoid placing it under pillows
  or blankets.

  ## Privacy

  Hush Log does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All audio recordings remain on your device.
